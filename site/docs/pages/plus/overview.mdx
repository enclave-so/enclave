# Enclave Plus

Enclave Plus is a subscription-based feature that enhances the onboarding experience for your users. It includes the following features:

- [**Boosts**](/plus/boosts.mdx): Increase user retention by showcasing your app in the "Boosted by" section of the user's wallet.
- **Gas Sponsoring (Coming Soon)**: Cover gas fees for your users. This feature is useful for onboarding new users and increasing conversion.

:::info[Lifetime Price]
This is a limited-time offer. If you start a subscription now, you will lock in a lifetime price of $50 per month, even if we increase the price later.
:::
